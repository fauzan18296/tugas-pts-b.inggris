Recipe 4
Koci-Koci Mutiara

Ingredients:

- 100 gr sago pearls
- 1 liter of water
- 4 tbsp sugar
- 65 ml of coconut milk
- food coloring to taste
- 130 grams of sugar
- 95 gr hunkwe flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 700 ml of coconut milk
- 1 sheet of pandan leaves

Steps:

- boil water, add 100 grams of pearl sago cover and boil for 5 minutes
- after 5 minutes turn off the heat and let stand for 30 minutes
- after 30 minutes stir briefly then boil again for 7 minutes after boiling for 7 minutes turn off the heat and let stand for 20 minutes
- after letting it sit for 20 minutes, add 4 tablespoons of sugar, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, mix well, 65 ml of coconut milk
- cook, stirring constantly, until it boils and thickens, then divide into 3 bowls, add enough pasta, stir well
- 130 gr of sugar, 95 gr of hunkwe flour, 1/4 tsp gram, 700 ml of coconut milk, add gradually, mix well 1 pandan leaf
- cook while stirring constantly until thickened and bubbling
- prepare opp plastic 11 x 11 side scissors
- Enter sago pearls to taste
- put enough hunkwe dough, do the same way until it's finished
